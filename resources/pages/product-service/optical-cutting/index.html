Optical material die cutting

Our optical material die cutting services are designed to meet the precise requirements of modern electronic devices. We specialize in crafting high-performance components for applications such as temporary protection, sapphire lapping, optical clear adhesive (OCA) processing, light management, EMI shielding, and battery bonding.

Utilizing advanced materials like protection films, thin flowable OCA, conductive tapes, EMI absorbers, and copper foils, we enhance product functionality in areas like grounding, heat spreading, NFC optimization, and low surface energy bonding. These solutions are tailored to enable seamless integration into devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other consumer electronics, ensuring optimal durability and performance.
